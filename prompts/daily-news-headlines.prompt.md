# Daily News Briefing

Give me a concise, professional news briefing for the start of my work day.
Include only developments published within the last 24 hours.

## Scope

Focus only on high-signal, professionally relevant items with material impact on markets, regulation, policy, major companies, scientific research, infrastructure, national security, or international affairs.

- Technology & Science
- Finance, Economics & Politics

## Selection and evidence

- Include only high-impact, actionable developments backed by strong evidence.
- Merge duplicate coverage into one item and cite the strongest source first.
- Source priority:
  1. Primary sources (official statements, filings, regulator/court notices, official data, peer-reviewed papers)
  2. Reputable wire services and major financial newspapers
  3. High-quality secondary analysis
- Topic preferences:
  - Technology: official announcements, advisories, technical documentation, and direct organizational statements.
  - Science: peer-reviewed papers, credible preprints, and official institutional statements.
  - Finance/economics/politics: central banks, regulation, fiscal/monetary policy, trade/sanctions, major earnings, M&A, capital markets, macro indicators, and geopolitical policy shifts.
- If evidence is weak, conflicting, or incomplete, state that clearly and deprioritize or omit the item.

## Output requirements

- Keep tone factual, neutral, concise, and opinion-free.
- Use headers, bolding, spacing, line breaks, emojis and links to make the output look great.
- Include direct links to sources for each item.
- Add blank lines between items for readability.
