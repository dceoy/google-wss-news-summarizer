Give me a concise, professional news briefing for the start of my work day. Include only developments published within the last 24 hours.

## Scope

Focus only on high-signal, professionally relevant items with material impact on markets, regulation, policy, major companies, scientific research, infrastructure, national security, or international affairs.

- 💻 Technology & Science
- 📊 Finance, Economics & Politics

Exclude unverified social media claims or weakly sourced breaking reports.

## Selection and evidence

- Prioritize important, actionable, evidence-backed developments.
- Merge duplicate coverage into one item and cite the strongest source first.
- Prefer sources in this order:
  1. Primary sources (official statements, filings, regulator/court notices, official data, peer-reviewed papers)
  2. Reputable wire services and major financial newspapers
  3. High-quality secondary analysis
- For technology: prefer official announcements, filings, advisories, technical documentation, and direct organizational statements.
- For science: prefer peer-reviewed papers, credible preprints, and official institutional statements.
- For finance/economics/politics: prioritize central banks, inflation/interest rates, regulation, fiscal policy, trade/sanctions, major earnings, M&A, capital markets, macro indicators, and geopolitical policy shifts.
- If evidence is weak, conflicting, or incomplete, state that clearly and deprioritize or omit the item.

## Source rules

- Include 1 to 3 sources per item.
- Use direct URLs only (no homepages).
- Do not fabricate citation details; omit unavailable fields.

Citation templates:

- News / official document:
  [1] Author or Organization. "Title." Publication or Issuing Body. Published YYYY-MM-DD. Direct URL
- Scientific paper:
  [1] Author(s). "Paper title." Journal. Year;Volume(Issue):Pages. DOI. Direct URL
- Preprint:
  [1] Author(s). "Paper title." Preprint (e.g. arXiv / bioRxiv / medRxiv). Posted YYYY-MM-DD. Direct URL
- Official filing:
  [1] Organization. "Filing / release title." Filing type or agency. Published YYYY-MM-DD. Direct URL

## Output

- No intro or conversational preamble.
- Use only these headers and omit empty sections:
  - 💻 Technology & Science
  - 📊 Finance, Economics & Politics
- Use bullet points under each header.
- Do not repeat the same event in multiple sections.
- Keep tone factual, neutral, concise, and opinion-free.

For each bullet point, use this structure exactly:

- **English:** Headline — 1–2 sentence factual summary.
  **Why it matters:** 1 sentence on practical significance (market / policy / industry / scientific relevance).
  **日本語:** 見出し — 1〜2文の事実ベース要約。
  **重要性:** 実務上の重要性を1文で記載。
  **Sources:**
  [1] ...
  [2] ...

## Final checks

- Verify last-24-hours compliance, duplicate events, source quality, and citation dates.
